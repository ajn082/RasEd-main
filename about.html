WHO WE ARE

Welcome to RasEd Academy. We're a dedicated group of high school students excited to spread knowledge about engineering to your child! At RasEd, we recognize the critical role that engineering plays in shaping the world around us. Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence they need to thrive in the dynamic field of engineering and STEM. At RasEd, we have very knowledgeable, curious, and dedicated educators with a passion for teaching for the one goal of making your child become the best version of themselves. We believe in hands-on and project based learning that will use real world examples and practices that will help your child become creative and develop problem-solving skills essential for the future of engineering.
